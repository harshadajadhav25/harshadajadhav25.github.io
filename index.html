Customer Churn Prediction & Analytics

Python • Pandas • Scikit-learn • Machine Learning • Streamlit • KPI Analysis

Developed an end-to-end customer churn analytics and machine learning solution to identify high-risk customers and support retention strategies. Analyzed customer behavior and business KPIs, identifying a 19.73% churn rate, 2,277 high-risk customers, and approximately $310K in revenue at risk. Built an interactive Streamlit application for churn prediction, customer risk assessment, KPI monitoring, and retention recommendations.
